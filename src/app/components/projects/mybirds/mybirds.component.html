Birding
When I was twenty four, I lived at the border between the city of Haarlem and the dunes. One sunny day I was driving back home and saw three buzzards circling a hundred meters away from my home and only about 50 meter high.
This was the first time I became aware birds of prey were so close around me. They looked to gracious and awkwardly bigger then I would have guessed. So, hoping to see them again I was trying to spot something everytime I looked in the direction I saw them that first time. Soon I realised they were flying there all the time. I just never noticed
And as easy as that my love for birds was born...
