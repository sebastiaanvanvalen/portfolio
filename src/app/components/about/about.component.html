My name is Sebastiaan, {{age}}, and currently living in Haarlem (Netherlands).
I started programming in 2019. Without any programming experience I was curious about the techniques and the way this work was done. I focused on webdesign and making websites and applications for browsers. I started with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. During a year of working together with my friend a neighbour I got to experiment with jQuery, Apache, MySQL, MariaDB and PHP as I made a application for the cafe where I worked. As a learning assignment, my neighbour asked me to transform some components of his application into VueJs/VUEX. This gave me the opportunity to work with a framework for the first time.
After I experimented with Angular and sticked with it untill now. My goal is to also make one project in React. This way I have at least minimal experience with these three frameworks. But for now my goal is to get a pro with Angular.
